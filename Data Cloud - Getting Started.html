In the 'Quick Find' box of the Setup screen, search for 'Permission Sets' and click Permission Sets
Click on the Data Cloud Admin permission set to open it
Click Manage Assignments
 Click Add Assignment
Check the box next to your user's name
Click Next
Click Assign
Click Done
Refresh your browser

In the 'Quick Find' box of the Setup screen, search for 'Data Cloud' and click Data Cloud > Setup

Click Get Started
When all four items have a green check, setup is complete
